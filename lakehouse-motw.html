The Story

Three spreads, side by side. Tzatziki — Greek yogurt with cucumber, dill, and garlic. Muhammara — roasted red peppers, cayenne, and feta. Baba Ghanoush — roasted eggplant, smoky and silky.

Served with warm pita and a glass of mixed vegetables for dipping. Built to share, built to start a meal, and built so every bite tastes different from the last.
