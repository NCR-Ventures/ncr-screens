Zucchini Fritti
"Enough for the Table" · Fried Zucchini Straws
The Story

Cut thin, dredged in seasoned flour, fried until the outside snaps and the inside stays tender. Showered with Parmigiano Reggiano and parsley while still hot. The cone is built for sharing — pull, dip, repeat.

Two sauces, two directions. Lemon aioli for the bright, garlicky finish. Marinara for the classic Italian dunk. Served at $10 — built to land in the middle of the table the moment everyone sits down.

Fun Fact · Little Gourds
The word zucchini is the Italian plural of zucchino — literally "little gourd," from zucca, the Italian word for squash. Italians have been frying them this way for as long as anyone can remember.
