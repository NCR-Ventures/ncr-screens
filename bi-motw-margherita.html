Pizza Margherita
Pizza Napoletana · A Neapolitan Classic
The Style

A true Neapolitan pizza — thin at the center with a puffed, blistered cornicione from a 900°F wood-fired oven. Those charred leopard spots are the mark of an authentic Neapolitan bake. Crisp on the outside, soft and pliable within.

The philosophy is restraint. Three toppings, nothing more: San Marzano tomato, fior di latte mozzarella, fresh basil, and a finish of extra-virgin olive oil. Balance over abundance.

The Legend · Naples, 1889
When Queen Margherita of Savoy visited Naples, pizzaiolo Raffaele Esposito crafted a pie with the three colors of the newly unified Italian flag — green basil, white mozzarella, red tomato. He named it in her honor. The story is part legend, part truth — but the pizza is the real thing.
