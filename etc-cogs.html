PERIOD 4 / WEEK 4 — SUBMITTED 4.27.26

Good week all around — came just shy of budget for the week and finished $11.5K below for the period, a stark improvement from the $50K miss last period. Business continues to pick up as the weather warms. Fridays and Saturdays holding strong; pushing weekday specials to keep foot traffic high.


Wine cost came back elevated due to volume of spritz sales driving a prosecco purchase from the store. Continuing to drive pour consistency and glass sales discipline. Will leverage dead-stock white wines for happy hour and build premium specials around Mother's Day.


Focus this week: Mother's Day and graduation week prep, continued hiring and training, and getting the pre-shift board installed today.
