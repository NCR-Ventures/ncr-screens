NCR Ventures 3 Opportunities to Be Better Spring Week — Patios Are Open
5 of 6
1
The Check-Back Is Slipping
Too many reviews about servers disappearing after food drops. No drink refills. No dessert offer. Guests watching other tables get served around them. Two-bite check-back. Every table. Every time. We're leaving money and hospitality on the table every time we miss it.
2
Manager Table Touches Can't Be "Ok."
A guest told a manager the service was bad. The manager said "ok" and took the plates. That review went online. A table touch is a chance to fix the night and save the relationship. If a guest hands us a problem, we own it and make it right. "Ok" is a one-star answer.
3
Special Occasions Are Getting Missed
A birthday noted on the reservation — no acknowledgment. A 5-year anniversary — nothing. When guests tell us in advance it's a special night, that's a gift. Pull occasion flags off the res sheet at pre-shift. Every time. These are easy hospitality wins we're throwing away.
