NCR Ventures 3 Opportunities to Be Better 🌷 Week Ending 5.10 — Mother's Day Weekend 🌷
5 of 5
1
Birthday Flags Slipped Through Two Weeks in a Row
A guest specifically wrote "daughter's birthday" on her reservation. Showed up. Got nothing. "Everything else was perfect... but it wasn't acknowledged." When the guest tells us in advance, that's a layup. Pre-shift: read every reservation note out loud. Assign one person to own the moment. Every flag gets touched.
2
The 15-Minute No-Greet Is a Five-Star Deal-Breaker
A guest sat for 15 minutes with no greeting, no water, no acknowledgment. The server was nearby — joking with coworkers. The guest wrote: "We will not be returning." Food was good. Doesn't matter. The first three minutes at the table set the entire review. Greet within 60 seconds. Every table. Every shift.
3
"You Need to Leave, We Need the Table"
A party of 8 paid their check at 8:15 and was told they needed to leave so the table could turn. That's not turn time. That's eviction. The right move is to invite them to the bar for a drink while we reset. The wrong move makes its way onto Yelp. Coach the language. Coach the move.
