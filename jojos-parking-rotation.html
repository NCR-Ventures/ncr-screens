How to Get Your Free Permit
1
Pick up the Parking Permit Application from a manager.
2
Have a manager sign it as proof of employment.
3
Drop it off at the Chagrin Falls Police Station or email admin@chagrin-falls.org. Hang tag issued on the spot.
4
Hang the permit from your rearview mirror any shift you park in a yellow lot. Renew seasonally.
