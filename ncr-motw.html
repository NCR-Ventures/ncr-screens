The Dish

Pan-seared crispy chicken finished with a classic beurre blanc — French butter sauce, bright with white wine and shallot. Topped with an arugula salad in lemon vinaigrette and shaved Parmesan.

Lemon squeezed tableside ties it all together — peppery greens, rich butter sauce, crisp golden chicken. Built for the guest who wants something feels-good but doesn't weigh them down.
