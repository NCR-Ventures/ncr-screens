Prime Rib Sandwich
Thin-Sliced · Au Jus · Onion Straws · 24
The Story

Thin-sliced prime rib piled into a fresh "On the Rise" baguette with melted provolone, sharp horseradish cream, and a tangle of crispy onion straws. Served with house au jus for dipping and a side of french fries.

Classic, generous, and built for the dip. The horseradish cream cuts the richness, the onion straws bring the crunch, and the bread holds up to the jus all the way through the last bite.
