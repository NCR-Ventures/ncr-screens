Bomba Sauce
A Calabrian Chili Spread
1 What It Is

The Basics

  • A spicy, fermented Italian condiment from Calabria.
  • Made from crushed Calabrian chiles, oil, and sometimes vegetables.
2 Flavor Profile

How It Tastes

  • Bold, savory, smoky heat.
  • Slightly tangy with a slow-building spice.
3 How to Describe It

Server Pitch

  • "A Calabrian chili spread with rich, layered heat."
  • "Great for guests who enjoy a spicy, flavorful kick."
4 Common Uses

Where It Lands

  • Pizza & pasta
  • Sandwiches & eggs
  • Mixed into dips, mayo, or hummus
The Pitch
Versatile as a spread, topping, or sauce enhancer.
