Why It Drinks So Well

The beauty of a red blend is freedom — the winemaker is not restricted by a single varietal and can build the best wine possible from the best fruit available.

Annabella is a Cabernet-based blend sourced from both Napa and California vineyards. Big fruit on the front, soft on the finish, easy to drink on its own or with a steak, a burger, or a bowl of pasta.

If a guest is looking for a smooth, approachable red that overdelivers for the price — pour Annabella with confidence.
