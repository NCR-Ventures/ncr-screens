Hand Cut Craft Ice — Bar Service Standard
Applies To: All NCR Ventures Bar Locations  |  Effective 2026
SOP 024
Craft Ice
The Standard
Service Rules
Auto-Included — High-End Pours
Any Bourbon, Scotch, Cognac, or Tequila priced over $16 ordered on the rocks receives a craft ice cube automatically. Served in a wash glass. One 2" cube. 2oz pour. No need to ask.
Guest-Requested — Well or Call Pours
Craft ice available at $1.00 upcharge. Ring "Craft Cube" on the Liquor Preps screen in POS. Must be rung every time — no exceptions. No self-comps without manager approval.
⚠ NEVER Used For
Non-alcoholic beverages · Staff drinks of any kind · Cocktails, mixers, shaken or stirred drinks · Personal use · Any well or call pour without the $1.00 ring-in. Misuse = cost control violation.
Glassware Standard
Wash glass — clean, polished, no water spots. Cube goes in first, then the pour. Never pre-load craft ice into glasses and let them sit. Cut or pull to order only.
Shift Checklist
☐Craft ice container stocked and separate from standard ice at bar setup
☐Wash glasses polished and staged for premium pours
☐Confirmed which spirits on tonight's menu are priced over $16
☐"Craft Cube" modifier confirmed active in POS liquor preps screen
☐Any low or cloudy craft ice reported to MOD before service
☐No craft ice used for non-alcoholic beverages or staff drinks
☐End of shift: craft ice container covered and stored properly
"Craft ice is a hospitality tool, not a perk. When used correctly — in a polished wash glass, with a premium spirit, presented intentionally — it elevates the guest's experience and signals that this bar takes what it does seriously."
